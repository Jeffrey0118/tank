Tank Battle

This is a top-down game,There are two tanks in the game, one for the enemy and one for the player. In the course of the game, the player needs to avoid the enemy tanks and fire shells to destroy the enemy tanks in order to win.

The Key Descriptions
Move up ↑
Move down ↓
Move left ←
Move right →
Firing bullets space

Special reminders !!!!

1: Once you click into the game, keep your tank as far away from the enemy tank as possible so that you have more chances to destroy the other side with artillery shells.

2: The enemy tank moves slower than the player tank and the bullets run slower, so you can use the gap between shell launches to fire shells at the enemy.

3: Only one of the arrow keys can be pressed at the same time, pressing two arrow keys at the same time will only move in the direction of the first arrow key pressed by default

4: As I've set the tank to only move up and down and left and right, the enemy tank isn't chasing the player very fast, but players still need to be aware that the tank can fire bullets!
